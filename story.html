OUR STORY

Fatima Alfadhel was just a young girl, when she started infatuating over Abayas and their fabric and styles, every tiny details held so much importance to her, hence she customized Abayas for herself leading attractions and requests from all around. Her statement; SIMPLE yet BOLD, CLASSIC yet COMOFRTABLE, her Belief; “that through Style you can be whatever you want to be” With all her devotion Her dream and Love for Fashion ushered her into creating her own unique styles of Abayas for the world that needs beauty in their attire. Abaya for her, is the core impression that a person leaves on one’s mind that lasts longer and forever in your wardrobe. 2020 it came true, THE KEY is a brand of fascinating Abayas, Like its Title “The key ” that is inspired from the the famous qoute by Bruce lee , The key to immortality is first living a life worth remembring . It is the true form of words into a clothing brand, that what inspires the Designer. SHOP NOW Home page
